HURRY UP! Read this quick to know how the test works


Each Test consists of 15 questions and each question has 4 options out of which one is correct. Each correct answer will give you Positive () Score depending on the question level and each wrong answer will deduct -5 points from your account and also will close the test automatically. Although you can quit and -5 points will not be deducted then. But remember you can only quit after attempting atleast 5 questions There are 3 levels of questions in the test and each level has different points.
Level 'E' (Easy) - 3 points | 6 questions
Level 'M' (Medium) - 5 points | 5 questions
Level 'H' (Hard) - 7 points | 4 questions
Easy Question has a time limit of 30 seconds. Medium Question has a time limit of 35 seconds. Hard Question has a time limit of 45 seconds. If the time is up and no option is selected by that period then the test will automatically close giving you the points of all the correct answers you provided and will also deduct -5 points from your account.
